Hi there! I am an incoming MS in Quantitative Economics candidate at NYU, Stern School of Business. Until then, I will be finishing up my bachelor's degree in Honours in Economics at McGill University, in Montreal, Canada. My main research interests lie in microeconomics, economic networks and econometrics. I'm also particularly interested in applications to labour and development economics.

In the Fall of 2022, I worked as a part-time Research Assistant to Prof. Hassan Benchekroun at McGill University, focusing on the private provision of public goods, in the context of charitable fundraising. Prior to that, I worked at The Decision Lab, a behavioural-science think tank and consulting firm. As a summer associate, I contributed to a consulting project whose purpose was to reduce alcohol abuse in South Africa through behavioural nudges.
