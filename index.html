Ranking Teams, Working Paper

A principal wishes to rank and reward teams in an organization, modelled by cliques in a graph. Agents in the organizational network perfectly observe the ranking of the teams they are part of and can report on the local ranking of their teams. We show that the only network structures that guarantee a complete ranking of teams are the triangle and the star graph. Unless the organizational network is a star, the principal cannot design an ex-post incentive compatible and efficient team ranking mechanism.
